###### gepub
---



```usage.rb
require 'rubygems'
require 'gepub'

book = GEPUB::Book.new
book.primary_identifier('http://tkgcci.com/bookid_in_url', '', '')
book.language = 'ja'

book.add_title 'GEPUBtext',
                title_type: GEPUB::TITLE_TYPE::MAIN,
                lang: 'ja',
                file_as: 'GEPUB text',
                display_seq: 1,
                alternates: {
                        '' => '',}



```
